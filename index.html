about me

I am a graduating Computer Science Major from Texas A&M University with a Minor in Sociology.

While at A&M, I interned with PepsiCo’s IT department and worked an 8-month co-op/internship with American Airlines’ Business Technology department. During the school semesters, I also enjoyed working as a peer teacher for A&M’s introductory Python course.

When I wasn’t working or doing school work, you could find me in meetings, hangouts, or roadtrips with my favorite humans from my two organizations - MSC Aggie Leaders of Tomorrow (ALOT) and Fish Camp.

Now, with COVID limiting large gatherings, you can find me re-watching Avatar: The Last Airbender, trying to take my cat for walks, journaling, or maybe even developing a website.
